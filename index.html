Featuring Honkai Star Rail

Honkai Star Rail

Honkai: Star Rail is a free-to-play, turn-based role-playing game developed and published by miHoYo Co., Ltd. Outside of the Chinese Mainland, the publisher is miHoYo's subsidiary, Cognosphere Pte., Ltd. d/b/a HoYoverse.

The game has a space-themed limited open world environment and turn-based combat system, with a gacha monetization system for players to obtain Characters, Light Cones, and other resources. The game can only be played with an internet connection.

It is the fourth installment in the Honkai series, following Honkai Impact 3rd, and uses both new characters and alternate versions of existing characters from its predecessors.

The game released worldwide on April 26, 2023, for iOS, Android, and Microsoft Windows. It released for PlayStation 5 on October 11th, 2023 globally.
